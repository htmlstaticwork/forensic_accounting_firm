Forensic Glossary

Understanding the language of financial litigation.

Benford's Law

A mathematical principle used to detect anomalies in naturally occurring data sets, frequently used to spot fake invoices.

Asset Dissipation

The intentional squandering or hiding of marital or corporate assets to prevent distribution during litigation.

Kiting

A form of check fraud involving the leveraging of the float time between banks to create false balances.

Kovel Agreement

An arrangement extending attorney-client privilege to the retained forensic accountant.
